About

Capability statement

Typhon Solutions Pty Ltd is an Australian specialist in automation systems, ICT and analytics.

We provide professional engineering and project management services in the areas of automatic control, electronic security, cloud infrastructure, middleware, communications, workflow integration, asset management and exploitation of big data. We also supply, install and commission associated data centre, plant and electrical equipment.

Our technologies and solutions enable our customers to automate and manage their businesses so that they can focus on what they do best.

Impartiality

We openly provide both consulting and contracted engineering services. We have a strict impartiality policy on consulting to protect stakeholders from conflicts of interest. Contact us to view our detailed policy.
